Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

This recipe will show you how to make a regular ol peanut butter jelly. As a parent of a growing child, This recipe is a all time favorite around the house. The recipe is a staple and a easy 1 2 snack for the kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of sliced bread
  • jar of peanut butter
  • jar of grape jelly
  1. Take out your slices of bread and lay them flat on a plate
  2. Cover one of the slices of bread thoroughly in grape jelly
  3. Cover the other slice of bread in peanut Butter
  4. Combine the two into a unique new form.
  5. Enjoy! BD
